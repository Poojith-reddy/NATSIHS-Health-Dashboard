# Screenshots

Place your Tableau dashboard screenshots here with the following naming convention:

- `01_time_series_trends.png` — Health Trends Over Time by Remoteness (line chart)
- `02_detailed_remoteness.png` — Detailed Health Conditions by Remoteness Area (bar chart / heat map)
- `03_health_service_use.png` — Health Service Utilisation (horizontal bar chart)
- `04_qld_vs_national.png` — Queensland vs National Comparison (side-by-side bar chart)
- `05_full_dashboard.png` — Complete Interactive Dashboard (combined view)

## How to Export Screenshots from Tableau

1. Open your dashboard/worksheet in Tableau
2. Go to **Dashboard** (or **Worksheet**) → **Export Image...**
3. Choose **PNG** format
4. Set resolution to at least **1920 x 1080** for clarity
5. Save with the naming convention above
